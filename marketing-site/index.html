About Us

Pretium Investments LLC is a real estate investment consultancy headquartered in Virginia. We specialize in acquiring, rehabilitating and selling real estate while providing comprehensive bookkeeping, financial reporting and advisory services to our clients.

Guided by our core values of transparency, integrity and performance, we leverage technology to deliver efficient operations, insightful data and exceptional customer experiences. Our mission is to empower individuals and small businesses to build wealth through real estate and financial literacy.

Our Services

Investment & Acquisition

Identification and acquisition of undervalued properties with strong ROI potential in the Virginia region and beyond.

Rehabilitation & Sales

Comprehensive property rehabilitation, project management and sales strategies to maximize value.

Financial Reporting

Robust bookkeeping, dashboards and financial statements delivered through our secure client portal.

Education & Courses

On-demand courses and training programs designed to build financial literacy and investment expertise.
